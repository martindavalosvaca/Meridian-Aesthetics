Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your journey

Yes! Hospital Metropolitano is the only JCI-accredited hospital in Ecuador. JCI (Joint Commission International) is the gold standard for healthcare quality and patient safety worldwide. You can verify this accreditation directly on their website.
Most patients stay 10-14 days. This includes 1-2 days pre-surgery for final evaluations, the surgery day, and 7-10 days of recovery before flying home. Your surgeon will provide specific recommendations based on your procedures.
Absolutely! We encourage patients to bring a companion. Our Premium and VIP packages include guest accommodation. Your companion can stay with you at the recovery house and provide additional support during your healing.
Our surgeons use advanced techniques to minimize scarring. Incisions are strategically placed in natural body contours. We provide scar management protocols including silicone sheets and creams. Most scars fade significantly within 6-12 months.
Complication insurance is included in our Premium and VIP packages. This covers any unexpected medical costs related to your surgery for up to 30 days post-procedure. Essential package patients can add this coverage for an additional fee.
Light activities can typically resume after 2 weeks. Most patients return to work (desk jobs) after 2-3 weeks. Exercise and heavy lifting should wait 6-8 weeks. Your surgeon will provide a personalized recovery timeline based on your specific procedures.
